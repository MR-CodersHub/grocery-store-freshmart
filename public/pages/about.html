What drives us

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To make farm-fresh, certified organic food accessible and affordable for every family. We eliminate barriers between growers and eaters by building a transparent, sustainable supply chain.

Our Vision

A world where every community has direct access to fresh, locally-grown food — and where farming is a sustainable livelihood, not a dying profession.

Our Values

Transparency, sustainability, community, and quality guide every decision we make. We treat our farmers as partners and our customers as neighbors.

✓
Radical Transparency
We show you exactly where your food comes from — farm names, locations, growing practices, and fair pricing breakdowns.
✓
Zero-Waste Promise
100% compostable packaging, optimized delivery routes, and food waste reduction programs with every order.
✓
Fair Farmer Partnerships
We pay above-market rates and commit to long-term contracts so our partner farms can invest in sustainable practices.
✓
Community First
10% of profits fund school meal programs and urban garden initiatives in underserved neighborhoods.

Our journey

How We Grew

2018

The Idea Was Born

Elena Rodriguez left her food science career and launched FreshMart from her garage, delivering to 50 families in the neighborhood.

2019

First Farm Partnerships

Signed contracts with 15 local farms. Launched the FreshMart app and expanded delivery to 500+ families across the metro area.

2020

Essential During the Crisis

When the world shut down, we kept delivering. Grew 300% in one year, reaching 5,000+ families and adding contactless delivery.

2022

Organic Certification & Meal Kits

Achieved USDA Organic partnership certification. Launched chef-designed Meal Kit service. 40+ farm partners now in the network.

2024

Going National

Expanded to 3 new regions. 80+ farm partners, 15,000+ families served. Launched Pro and Family subscription tiers.

2026

The Future Is Now

Zero-waste packaging initiative underway. AI-powered freshness tracking. Goal: 100,000 families and carbon-negative operations by 2028.
