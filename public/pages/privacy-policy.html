1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our grocery delivery services. The types of information we gather include:

Personal Information

  • • Account details: Name, email address, phone number, and password when you create an account.
  • • Payment information: Credit/debit card numbers, billing address, and payment confirmation. Card details are processed securely by our PCI-compliant payment partners and are never stored on our servers.
  • • Delivery address: Physical addresses where you want your orders delivered, including saved and frequently used locations.

Usage Information

  • • Order history: Products you've browsed, purchased, or added to your cart, along with timestamps and order values.
  • • Device information: Browser type, operating system, device type, IP address, and unique device identifiers.
  • • Location data: Approximate geographic location derived from your IP address or, with your consent, precise GPS location for delivery optimization.

Communications

  • • Customer support messages, feedback, and reviews you submit through our platform or email.
  • • Newsletter subscription preferences and marketing communication choices.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Order Fulfillment

Processing, preparing, and delivering your grocery orders. Sending order confirmations, delivery updates, and receipts.

Account Management

Creating and maintaining your account, managing your preferences, saving delivery addresses, and tracking your loyalty rewards.

Personalization

Recommending products based on your purchase history, customizing your shopping experience, and curating personalized deals.

Communication

Sending service-related messages, marketing communications (with your consent), responding to your inquiries, and notifying you about deals.

Improvement & Analytics

Analyzing usage patterns, conducting A/B tests, improving our platform features, and optimizing delivery routes for efficiency.

Safety & Legal

Detecting and preventing fraud, ensuring platform security, complying with legal obligations, and enforcing our terms of service.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data in the following limited circumstances:

1

Delivery Partners

We share your name, delivery address, and phone number with our delivery partners solely to fulfill your order. They are contractually bound to use this information only for delivery purposes.

2

Payment Processors

Payment card details are transmitted to PCI-compliant payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal) to authorize and complete transactions. We never store full card numbers.

3

Service Providers

Trusted third-party vendors who assist with hosting, analytics, email delivery, and customer support. These providers access only the minimum data necessary and are bound by strict data protection agreements.

4

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of FreshMart, our customers, or the public.

5

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your personal information may be transferred. We will notify you of any such change and any choices you may have regarding your data.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our platform.

Essential Cookies

Required for core functionality such as keeping you logged in, processing your cart, and maintaining session state. These cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our site — which pages are most popular, where users encounter errors, and how we can improve the experience. We use Google Analytics and similar tools.

Marketing Cookies

Used to deliver relevant advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness. These are only set with your explicit consent and can be managed through your cookie preferences.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie consent banner. Disabling essential cookies may impair site functionality.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement multiple layers of protection:

🔒

Encryption

All data is transmitted using TLS 1.3 encryption. Sensitive information at rest is encrypted using AES-256.

🛡️

Access Controls

Strict role-based access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access personal data, and all access is audited.

🔍

Regular Audits

We conduct regular security audits, penetration testing, and vulnerability assessments with third-party firms.

🚨

Incident Response

We maintain a comprehensive incident response plan and will notify affected users within 72 hours of discovering any data breach.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong, unique passwords for your account.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

✓

Access & Portability

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you in a structured, machine-readable format.

✓

Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information in your account.

✓

Deletion

Request deletion of your personal data, subject to certain legal retention requirements (e.g., transaction records for tax purposes).

✓

Opt-Out of Marketing

Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the link in the email or through your account settings.

✓

Withdraw Consent

Withdraw previously given consent for data processing at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@freshmart.com. We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

FreshMart's services are intended for users who are at least 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 16 without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information from our servers promptly.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at privacy@freshmart.com and we will remove the information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make material changes, we will:

  • • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
  • • Send an email notification to the address associated with your account.
  • • Display a prominent notice on our website for at least 30 days.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Your continued use of our services after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
